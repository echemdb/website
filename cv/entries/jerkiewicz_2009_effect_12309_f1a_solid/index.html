    echemdb identifier: jerkiewicz_2009_effect_12309_f1a_solid
    tags: BCV , UPD

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Pt(111) recorded in \(0.05 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ H2SO4 }\) + \(0.005 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ CuSO4*H2O }\) at a scan rate of \(50 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 1 in G. Jerkiewicz et al. Effect of temperature variation on the under-potential deposition of copper on Pt\(111\) in aqueous H2SO4. The Journal of Physical Chemistry C, 113\(28\):12309â€“12316, 2009.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Pt(111) electrode was prepared by:
    * Annealing the Pt(111) electrode in a H2/O2 flame. Cooling in Ar/H2 (2:1) gas atmosphere. Protecting the Pt(111) surface with droplet of high purity water.
