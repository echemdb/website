    Au(100) - \(0.1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ H3PO4 }\)

    echemdb identifier: cuesta_2000_the_5684_f3b_black
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Au(100) recorded in \(0.1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ H3PO4 }\) at a scan rate of \(10 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 2a in A. Cuesta et al. The adsorption of sulfate and phosphate on Au\(111\) and Au\(100\) electrodes: an in situ STM study. Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics, 2\(24\):5684–5690, 2000. URL: http://dx.doi.org/10.1039/B006464P, doi:10.1039/b006464p.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Au(100) electrode was prepared by:
    * Annealed in a hydrogen flame for 6 min.
    * Cooled down in nitrogen stream.
