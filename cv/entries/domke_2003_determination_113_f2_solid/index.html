    Pt(100) - \(0.1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ HClO4 }\)

    echemdb identifier: domke_2003_determination_113_f2_solid
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Pt(100) recorded in \(0.1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ HClO4 }\) at a scan rate of \(50 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 2a in K. Domke et al. Determination of the potentials of zero total charge of Pt\(100\) stepped surfaces in the [011] zone. Effect of the step density and anion adsorption. Journal of Electroanalytical Chemistry, 552:115–128, 2003.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Pt(100) electrode was prepared by:
    * heating for 10 s in a gas-oxygen flame
    * cooled in a reductive atmosphere (H2+Ar)

    Comment left by the curator on the published figure Lines are overlapping partly.
