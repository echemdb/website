    Pt(111) - \(0.5 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ H2SO4 }\)

    echemdb identifier: nishihara_1994_underpotential_75_f7a_solid
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Pt(111) recorded in \(0.5 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ H2SO4 }\) at a scan rate of \(20 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 7a in C. nishihara et al. Underpotential deposition of copper on Pt\(S\)-[n\(111\)×\(100\)] electrodes in sulfuric acid solution. Journal of Electroanalytical Chemistry, 386\(1\-2\):75–82, 1995.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Pt(111) electrode was prepared by:
    * Annealing in a hydrogen/air flame.
    * Quenched in hydrogen-bubbled water under an argon atmosphere.

    Comment left by the curator on the published figure Imprecise Y-axis labelling.
