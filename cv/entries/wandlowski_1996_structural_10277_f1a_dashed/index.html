    Au(100) - \(0.05 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ NaClO4 }\)

    echemdb identifier: wandlowski_1996_structural_10277_f1a_dashed
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Au(100) recorded in \(0.05 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ NaClO4 }\) at a scan rate of \(10 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 1a in T. Wandlowski et al. Structural and kinetic aspects of bromide adsorption on Au\(100\). The Journal of Physical Chemistry, 100\(24\):10277–10287, 1996.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Au(100) electrode was prepared by:
    * Electrodes were annealed and quenched in water. Measurements with hanging meniscus technique.
