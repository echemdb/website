    Au(111) - \(\ce{ EMMIm-TFSI }\)

    echemdb identifier: bonhote_1996_hydrophobic_1168_f7_solid
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Au(111) recorded in \(\ce{ EMMIm-TFSI }\) at a scan rate of \(50 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 7 in P. Bonhote et al. Hydrophobic, highly conductive ambient-temperature molten salts. Inorganic chemistry, 35\(5\):1168–1178, 1996.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Au(111) electrode was prepared by:
    * Electrochemical polishing and flame annealing.
