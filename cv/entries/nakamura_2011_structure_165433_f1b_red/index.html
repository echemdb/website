    Ag(100) - \(0.01 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ CsBr }\) + \(0.04 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ CsF }\)

    echemdb identifier: nakamura_2011_structure_165433_f1b_red
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Ag(100) recorded in \(0.01 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ CsBr }\) + \(0.04 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ CsF }\) at a scan rate of \(50 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 1b in M. Nakamura et al. Structure of the electrical double layer on Ag\(100\): Promotive effect of cationic species on Br adlayer formation. Physical Review B, 84\(16\):165433, 2011.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Ag(100) electrode was prepared by:
    * Etched with chromic acid.
    * Annealed in H2/Ar in an induction heating furnace.
