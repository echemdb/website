    Au(111) - \(5 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ H2SO4 }\)

    echemdb identifier: sato_2006_effect_725_f4c_red
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Au(111) recorded in \(5 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ H2SO4 }\) at a scan rate of \(5 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 4c in K. Sato et al. Effect of Sulfuric Acid Concentration on the Structure of Sulfate Adlayer on Au\(111\) Electrode. Electrochem. Commun., 8\(5\):725–730, May 2006. doi:https://doi.org/10.1016/j.elecom.2006.03.001.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Au(111) electrode was prepared by:
    * Annealed in a hydrogen flame for 1 min.
    * Quenched in ultrapure water.
