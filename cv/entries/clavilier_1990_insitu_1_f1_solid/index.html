    Pt(111) - \(0.5 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ H2SO4 }\)

    echemdb identifier: clavilier_1990_insitu_1_f1_solid
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Pt(111) recorded in \(0.5 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ H2SO4 }\) at a scan rate of \(50 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 1 in J. Clavilier et al. In situ probing of step and terrace sites on Pt \(S\)-[n \(111\)×\(111\)] electrodes. Chemical physics, 141\(1\):1–14, 1990.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Pt(111) electrode was prepared by:
    * heating in a gas flame
    * cooling in air to 300-200 °C
    * protecting the surface with a droplet of ultrapure water
