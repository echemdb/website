    Pt(100) - \(0.1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ HClO4 }\)

    echemdb identifier: gomez_2003_effect_228_f2_dotted
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Pt(100) recorded in \(0.1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ HClO4 }\) at a scan rate of \(50 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 2 in R. Gómez et al. Effect of temperature on hydrogen adsorption on Pt\(111\), Pt\(110\), and Pt\(100\) electrodes in 0.1 M HClO4. The Journal of Physical Chemistry B, 108\(1\):228–238, 2004.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Pt(100) electrode was prepared by:
    * heated in gas/air flame
    * cooled in H2/Ar
    * transfer with the surface protected by a droplet of ultrapure water
    * immersed under potential control
