    Ag(111) - \(0.1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ NaOH }\)

    echemdb identifier: jovic_1999_cyclic_247_f4_solid
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Ag(111) recorded in \(0.1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ NaOH }\) at a scan rate of \(50 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 4 in B. Jovic et al. Cyclic voltammetry on Ag\(111\) and Ag\(100\) faces in sodium hydroxide solutions. Electrochemistry communications, 1\(6\):247–251, 1999.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Ag(111) electrode was prepared by:
    * Ag crystal polished with fine emery paper and alumina.
    * Etched with cyanide and H2O2.

    Comment left by the curator on the published figure The large negative peak was not completely plotted in the figure.
