    Ag(poly) - \(1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ NaOH }\)

    echemdb identifier: rehim_1998_electrochemical_1103_f1_solid
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Ag(poly) recorded in \(1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ NaOH }\) at a scan rate of \(100 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 1 in S. S. Abd El Rehim et al. Electrochemical behaviour of a silver electrode in NaOH solutions. Monatshefte für Chemie/Chemical Monthly, 129\(11\):1103–1117, 1998.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.
