    Pt(775) - \(\ce{ HClO4 }\)

    echemdb identifier: taguchi_2007_electrochemical_6023_f2d_solid
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Pt(775) recorded in \(\ce{ HClO4 }\) at a scan rate of \(50 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 2d in S. Taguchi et al. Electrochemical reduction of nitrate on Pt \(S\)[n \(111\)×\(111\)] electrodes in perchloric acid solution. Electrochimica acta, 52\(19\):6023–6033, 2007.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Pt(775) electrode was prepared by:
    * flame-annealing
    * cooling in a gas stream of H2/Ar
    * quenching with ultra-pure water saturated by with H2/Ar gas
