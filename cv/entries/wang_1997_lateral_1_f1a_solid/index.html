    echemdb identifier: wang_1997_lateral_1_f1a_solid
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Ag(100) recorded in \(0.01 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ KBr }\) + \(0.04 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ KClO4 }\) at a scan rate of \(10 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 1a in J. Wang et al. Lateral interaction energy derived from Frumkin isotherm for c\(2x2\) Br/Ag\(100\). Technical Report, Brookhaven National Lab., Upton, NY \(United States\), 1997.
