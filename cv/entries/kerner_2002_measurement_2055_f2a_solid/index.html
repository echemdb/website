    Au(111) - \(0.1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ HClO4 }\)

    echemdb identifier: kerner_2002_measurement_2055_f2a_solid
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Au(111) recorded in \(0.1 \; \mathrm{M}\) \(\ce{ HClO4 }\) at a scan rate of \(10 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 2a in Z. Kerner et al. Measurement of adsorption rates of anions on Au\(111\) electrodes by impedance spectroscopy. Electrochimica acta, 47\(13\-14\):2055–2063, 2002.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    Preparation procedure not available.

    Comment left by the curator on the published figure For CV with KI, KClO4 is used instead of HClO4 (as for KCl, BrCl).
