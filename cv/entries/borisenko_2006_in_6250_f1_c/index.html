    Au(111) - \(\ce{ BMP-TFSI }\)

    echemdb identifier: borisenko_2006_in_6250_f1_c
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Au(111) recorded in \(\ce{ BMP-TFSI }\) at a scan rate of \(10 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 1 in N. Borisenko et al. In situ STM investigation of gold reconstruction and of silicon electrodeposition on Au\(111\) in the room temperature ionic liquid 1-butyl-1-methylpyrrolidinium bis\(trifluoromethylsulfonyl\)imide. The journal of physical chemistry B, 110\(12\):6250–6256, 2006.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Au(111) electrode was prepared by:
    * Annealed at 350 °C for 4h under vacuum.

    Comment left by the curator on the published figure Baseline of the y-axis was not given and therby it was selected by the curator. The original data is very noisy.
