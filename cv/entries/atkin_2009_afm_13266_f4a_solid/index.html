    Au(111) - \(\ce{ BMP-TFSI }\)

    echemdb identifier: atkin_2009_afm_13266_f4a_solid
    tags:

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Au(111) recorded in \(\ce{ BMP-TFSI }\) at a scan rate of \(10 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 4a in R. Atkin et al. Zein El Abedin. S., Hayes, R., Gasparotto, LHS, Borisenko, N., Endres, F.,“AFM and STM studies on the surface interaction of [BMP] TFSA and [EMIm] TFSA ionic liquids with Au \(III\)”, J. Phys. Chem. C, 113:13266–13272, 2009.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Au(111) electrode was prepared by:
    * Annealed at 350°C for 4h under vacuum in a quartz ampule.

    Comment left by the curator on the published figure A baseline for zero current was not available and hence was chosen by the curator. The actual data has more noise than the digitized data.
