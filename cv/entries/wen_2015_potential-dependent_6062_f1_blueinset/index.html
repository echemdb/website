    Au(111) - \(\ce{ BMP-TFSI }\)

    echemdb identifier: wen_2015_potential-dependent_6062_f1_blueinset
    tags: BCV

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Au(111) recorded in \(\ce{ BMP-TFSI }\) at a scan rate of \(10 \; \mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 1 in R. Wen et al. Potential-Dependent Adlayer Structure and Dynamics at the Ionic Liquid/Au\(111\) Interface: A Molecular-Scale In Situ Video-STM Study. Angewandte Chemie International Edition, 54\(20\):6062–6066, 2015.

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Au(111) electrode was prepared by:
    * Flame annealing in butane flame.
